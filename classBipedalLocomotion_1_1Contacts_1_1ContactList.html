Detailed Description

Class containing a list of contacts.

The contact are added such that the activation time is strictly growing. In addition, contacts cannot be overlapping. It represents a series of contact activations and deactivations of a single entity.

◆ forceSampleTime()

bool ContactList::forceSampleTime ( const std::chrono::nanoseconds &  dt)

Force the sample time of the contact list.

Parameters
dtThe new sample time.
Returns
true if the contact list has been correctly resampled.
Note
the activation time is rounded down to the nearest multiple of dt, while the deactivation time is rounded up to the nearest multiple of dt.
If the deactivation time of a contact in the list is equal to std::chrono::nanoseconds::max() it will not be rounded up.
Warning
This will change the sample time of all the contacts in the list. All the iterators to the contacts will be invalidated.
