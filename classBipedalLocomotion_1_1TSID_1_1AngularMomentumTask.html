Detailed Description

AngularMomentumTask is a concrete implementation of the TSIDLinearTask.

Please use this element if you want to control the Centroidal angular momentum of the robot. The task represents the following equation

\[ \sum \begin{bmatrix} S(p_{C_k} - x & I_{3 \times 3} \end{bmatrix} f_k = \dot{h} ^ * _\omega \]

where the suffix \(S\) is the skew operator. \(p_{C_k}\) is the position of the contact \(k\), \(x\) is the CoM position, \(f_k\) is the contact wrench expressed in mixed representation. Finally \(\dot{h} ^ * _\omega\) is the desired rate of change of the angular momentum. The angular momentum rate of change is computed by a Proportional controller in \(\mathbb{R}^3\).
