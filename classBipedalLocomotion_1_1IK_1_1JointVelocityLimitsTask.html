Detailed Description

JointVelocityLimitsTask is a concrete implementation of the Task.

Please use this element if you want to ensure that the generated joint velocity will consider the joints velocity limits. The task represents the following equation

\[ \dot{s}_{-} \preceq \begin{bmatrix} 0_6 & I_n \end{bmatrix} \nu \preceq \dot{s}_{+} \]

where \(0_6\) and \(I_n\) are the zero and the identity matrix. \(\dot{s} _{-}\) and \(\dot{s} _{+}\) are the lower and upper limits of the joints velocity.
